Purpose

Our target audience for the rafting site are people who likes adventure, people who wants to find new adventures and people who likes water sports.

Audience

  • Who is our primary target audience for the rafting website?

    Our website target audience is consists of people who likes adventure, enjoy doing outdoors activities, and people who enjoy water rafting.

  • How do our target audience members typically discover information about water rafting experiences?

    Our target audience discovers inforamation about water rafting experiences by searching through online websites like Google, posts from social media sites, and recommendations from family and friends.

  • What factors influence our target audience's decision to choose one water rafting prodiver over another?

    Our target audience's decision is influnce by knowinf if the rafting provider is ensuring their safety and follow guidelines for their protection, pricing, location, and feedback from past customers.
